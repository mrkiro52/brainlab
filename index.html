Laboratory
for Fundamental Research in Artificial Intelligence at MIPT

Advancing the mathematical foundations, algorithms, and theoretical principles of next-generation AI systems.

Our team unites researchers and students dedicated to advancing the fundamental foundations of artificial intelligence. We combine rigorous theory with collaborative research to drive scientific excellence and develop the next generation of AI experts.

Master's & PhD Programs

We offer specialized graduate programs in AI theory, machine learning, and computational mathematics with world-class faculty mentorship and cutting-edge research opportunities.

Advanced Courses

Deep learning architectures, reinforcement learning, probabilistic models, and optimization theory taught by leading researchers in the field of artificial intelligence.

Research Supervision

Individual mentorship from experienced researchers for bachelor's, master's, and doctoral theses in theoretical AI, neural networks, and computational intelligence.
