Areas of research

Deep Learning Theory

Investigating theoretical foundations of deep neural networks, convergence properties, and generalization bounds for modern architectures.

Optimization Algorithms

Developing and analyzing advanced optimization methods for training large-scale machine learning models with provable convergence guarantees.

Computer Vision

Advancing self-supervised learning, object detection, and semantic segmentation through novel architectures and training paradigms.

Natural Language Processing

Exploring transformer architectures, language understanding, and generation with focus on computational efficiency and interpretability.

Reinforcement Learning

Developing theoretical frameworks and practical algorithms for decision-making in complex, uncertain environments with limited data.

Explainable AI

Creating interpretable machine learning models and developing methods to understand and explain decisions of black-box AI systems.
