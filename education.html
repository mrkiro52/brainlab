Educational Programs

Comprehensive courses in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and deep learning. From fundamental concepts to cutting-edge research methods.

About the Course

This comprehensive introduction to deep learning covers fundamental concepts and practical applications. Students will learn about neural network architectures, backpropagation, optimization algorithms, and regularization techniques. The course includes hands-on projects with real-world datasets and modern deep learning frameworks.

Topics Covered

  • Neural Networks Fundamentals
  • Backpropagation and Gradient Descent
  • Convolutional Neural Networks
  • Recurrent Neural Networks and LSTMs
  • Optimization Techniques (Adam, RMSprop)
  • Regularization and Dropout
  • Batch Normalization
  • Transfer Learning
  • Introduction to PyTorch and TensorFlow
  • Practical Projects and Applications

  • Multi-Modal Learning (CLIP, ALIGN)

About the Course

About the Course

This course explores mathematical foundations and practical aspects of optimization algorithms used in machine learning. Students will gain deep understanding of convergence properties, adaptive methods, and second-order optimization techniques essential for training modern neural networks.

Topics Covered

  • Convex Optimization Basics
  • Gradient Descent Variants
  • Stochastic Gradient Descent
  • Momentum and Nesterov Acceleration
  • Adaptive Learning Rates (AdaGrad, RMSprop, Adam)
  • Second-Order Methods (Newton, L-BFGS)
  • Coordinate Descent
  • Proximal Methods
  • Convergence Analysis
  • Distributed Optimization

About the Course

An essential course for understanding and explaining AI model decisions. Students will learn various interpretability techniques, from feature importance to attention visualization, and how to build transparent and trustworthy machine learning systems that can be understood by both technical and non-technical stakeholders.

Topics Covered

  • Introduction to Interpretability
  • LIME and SHAP
  • Feature Importance and Permutation Tests
  • Attention Visualization
  • Saliency Maps and Grad-CAM
  • Counterfactual Explanations
  • Interpretable Model Architectures
  • Neural Network Pruning
  • Concept Activation Vectors
  • Fairness and Bias Detection
  • Case Studies in Healthcare and Finance
